Frequently Asked Questions

What is JoSAA Counselling?

+

JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) is the centralized counselling process for admission to IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other GFTIs based on JEE Main and Advanced ranks.

What is CSAB?

+

CSAB (Central Seat Allocation Board) conducts special rounds of counselling for the vacant seats left in NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs after all JoSAA rounds are completed.

Can I get an NIT with my rank?

+

Yes, depending on your category, home state quota, and choice filling strategy. Our expert counsellors analyze previous year cutoffs to predict and secure the best possible NIT for your rank.

Why use College Pilot?

+

College Pilot provides data-driven, personalized choice filling lists, one-to-one mentorship, and expert guidance till admission to ensure you don't make mistakes that could cost you a good college.

How does counselling work?

+

Once you enroll, we analyze your rank, create a personalized college shortlist, provide the exact choice filling order, and guide you through every round of seat allotment and physical reporting.
